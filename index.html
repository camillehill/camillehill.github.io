about me

When I discovered the field of front end and ux I thought I was just finding a career. Really I found place to combine all my interest in one because tech is everywhere -- literally. Now that all the mushy gushy is out the way...I'm just a person that happens to love technology and love people.

Like most college students, I think I wondered to each department of each college searching for the "perfect" major, thinking I had to settle for just one thing to do the REST OF MY LIFE! I went from music to medicine to personal training to teaching to computer science. What helpped me was instead of focusing on the title focusing on the impact: what career allowed me to make the impact and imprint I wanted on the world. I hope through my site, projects, and hopefully a conversation one day I'll be able to make an impact in the lives connected to me.
